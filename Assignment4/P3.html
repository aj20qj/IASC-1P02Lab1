DLC Main Game

Main Game Locations

Undead Asylum

Description

The Undead Asylum is the place where Undead are taken to await the end of the world. The Asylum Demon guards the exit to prevent Undead from escaping. It is said that one day a chosen undead will take pilgrammage from the asylum and travel to the land of the ancient lords...

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Asylum Demon 2,000 Mandatory
Stray Demon 20,000 Optional


Firelink Shrine

Description

Firelink Shrine is the place where the Chosen Undead starts their journey. This cozy place offers solace from the harsh outside world, and is where many of the NPCs will end up along their quests. This location connects to the Undead Burg, the Undead Parish, the Catacombs and the New Londo Ruins.



Undead Burg

Description

The Undead Burg is a smaller undead settlement on the outskirts of Lordran that has been overrun by hollows and demons. The player must make their way through the gauntlets of enemies before ultimately facing off against the Taurus Demon to reach the bridge to the Undead Parish.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Taurus Demon 3,000 Mandatory*
Capra Demon 3,000 Mandatory*

Mandatory* = Mandatory unless skipped with Master Key



Undead Parish

Description

Once the player crosses the Drake bridge to get in to the Undead Parish, their journey's difficulty takes a steep increase. The Chosen Undead must go face to face with an Iron Boar and the Baldur Knights on their approach to the Parish rooftops. Upon reaching the Parish, the player will be able to unlock a shortcut back to Firelink Shrine as well as an epic rooftop fight against the Bell Gargoyles. When they are finally defeated, the player may ring the first Bell of Awakening.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Bell Gargoyles 10,000 Mandatory


The Depths

Description

After the player goes through Lower Undead Burg and defeats the Capra Demon they will receive the key to the Depths. The player enters into a butcher's hideout before venturing lower and lower into the rat and slime infested sewer. When the Chosen Undead reaches the bottom they will be taking on the Gaping Dragon, an ancient dragon malformed by its gluttony. Once defeated, the Gaping Dragon will drop the Key to Blighttown to unlock the next area.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Gaping Dragon 20,000 Mandatory*

Mandatory* = Mandatory unless skipped with Master Key



Blighttown

Description

The descent into Blighttown is down a dark and dingy sewer hole, and the path is incredibly treacherous. The Chosen Undead must make their way down a series of rickety wooden footpaths hanging over the side of the massive ravine. Once the player has made their way down into the poisonous swamp, there are a few ways to go. The player will first venture into Quelaag's domain to ring the second Bell of Awakening, but there are pathways to both The Great Hollow and the Valley of the Drakes as well.



Quelaag's Domain

Description

Entering the egg-infested domain of Quelaag, the Chosen Undead must go up against the Chaos Witch Quelaag in her human/spider hybrid form. Once defeated, the player will gain access to the secret Daughter of Chaos bonfire and be able to ring the second bell of awakening.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Chaos Witch Quelaag 20,000 Mandatory


The Great Hollow

Description

To find the entrance to the Great Hollow, the Chosen Undead must walk up a tree root in the swamp and roll through two illusory walls. Once inside, the player must descend the vertical branches and pathways while avoiding the Basilisks and Mushrooms to safely make it to the bottom of the tree. From there, the player will venture onwards to one of the most breathtaking secrets in Dark Souls.



Ash Lake

Description

Once reaching the bottom of the Great Hollow, players walk out into an endless sea of trees and fog on a sandy beach. The player can encounter another hydra while finding rare items along the beach and around the trees that the player can reach. Waiting at the end is one of the Everlasting Stone Dragons, where the player can unlock the power of the Dragons.



Sens Fortress

Description

Once they ring the two bells of awakening, the player must venture to Sens Fortress to take on the maze of traps and deadly serpent enemies. After making their way across the narrow stone pathways and around the traps inside, the player must make a run across the rooftops to kill the giant that is bombarding it with flames. This results in the final test for the Chosen Undead going against the Iron Golem, which was created using the bone of an everlasting dragon. Once defeated, demon creatures in service to the gods will assist the player in their ascent to Anor Londo.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Iron Golem 40,000 Mandatory


Anor Londo

Description

The player makes their ascent to Anor Londo, the City of the Gods. While mostly vacant, the Chosen Undead must make their way past painting guardians on the rafters to unlock the mechanism allowing entrance to Gwyn's palace. Once there, the player must face their toughest opponents yet in the Silver Knights and their archers. Finally making their way to the inner sanctum, the player will face off against the deadly duo of Dragonslayer Ornstein and Executioner Smough. These two elite warriors will pose a signficiant challenge. Once defeated, the player will be granted audience with Gwynevere, the daughter of the great Lord Gwyn. She will award you with the Lordvessel, allowing fast travel to previous bonfires and the ability to travel to the four great souls to unlock the Kiln of the First Flame. The player can search for the great souls, make their way into the Painted World of Ariamis, or fight the secret boss Dark Sun Gwyndolin.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Dark Sun Gwyndolin 40,000 Optional
Ornstein & Smough 50,000 Mandatory


The Painted World of Ariamis

Description

After returning to the Undead Asylum to retrieve the doll left in the player's original cell, this large paiting in Anor Londo will suck the player inside, trapping them until they find the exit. This hollow world filled with dead or sickly creatures serves as a respite for the outcasts of the world. Once the player reaches Crossbreed Priscilla, they will have the option to fight her or leave peacefully. In either case, the Undead plunges down the plank at the back of the boss arena and returns to the real world.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Crossbreed Priscilla 30,000 Optional


Darkroot Garden

Description

The Darkroot Garden is home to ents and other forest creatures. Crumbled buildings remain around the area as well as Great Stone Knights with powerful magic spells. The player can optionally fight the Moonlight Butterfly to unlock the holy ember used for smithing, but the mandatory boss is Sif the Great Grey Wolf that can be found past the door that is opened with the Seal of Artorias. The player will receive the Covenant of Artorias ring which allows them to traverse the abyss and fight the Four Kings in New Londo later on.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Moonlight Butterfly 10,000 Optional
Great Grey Wolf Sif 40,000 Mandatory


Darkroot Basin

Description

The Darkroot Basin is a small side area accessed through Darkroot Garden or Undead Burg with the master key. This area has the well and Hydra area with crystal golems used to access the DLC as well as providing access to Darkroot Garden, Undead Burg, and a shortcut to Valley of the Drakes.



Valley of the Drakes

Description

The Valley of the Drakes is a connector area in Dark Souls with pathways to and from New Londo Ruins, Blighttown, Darkroot Basin, and Firelink Shrine. The valley boasts a lone bridge with a handful of drakes as well as an undead dragon on the cliffside guarding some useful items. The most important feature of the area is the large iron door to the New Londo Ruins, as once opened this door will allow the water to drain from the abandoned city.



New Londo Ruins

Description

To drain the water in New Londo, the player must use transient curses to be able to fight ghosts on the narrow pathways and buildings of the haunted town. Eventually the player will come across Ingward, Guardian of the Seal. The Chosen Undead will retrieve the key to the seal from him and unlock the door to the Valley of the Drakes to drain the water. Once drained, the player will be able to access lower New Londo and fight the Darkwraiths and monsters coming from the abyss. The player will eventually encounter the Four Kings of New Londo in the abyss after retrieving the Covenant of Artorias ring from Sif the Great Grey Wolf. Once the Four Kings are defeated, the player will receive their lord soul and be one step closer to accessing the Kiln of the First Flame.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Four Kings 60,000 Mandatory


The Duke's Archives

Description

Travelling up to the archives from Anor Londo will pit the player against two armoured boars in a narrow hallway, proving to be a tough test to enter. Once inside, the player will face off against Seath's channelers and several crystalized hollows to reach Seath's sanctum. The player will not be able to kill Seath thanks to his immortality crystal, and faces imprisonment in the archive tower. Once imprisoned, the player must take on snake men and Seath's horrific serpent creations to get the key to escape. When they finally get out, the player will be able to access the Crystal Cave and several previously locked areas with plenty of loot for magic users.



Crystal Cave

Description

Arguably one of the toughest areas in the game, this crystal cavern has invisible magic pathways only visible by small falling snowflakes over the path. The player must trust their gut and venture over gaping chasms as they fight strong golem enemies on the footpaths. Once they have gotten to the end of the invisible labyrinth, the Chosen Undead must destroy Seath's immortality crystal in order to fight him and put the crazed Dragon to rest.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Seath the Scaleless 60,000 Mandatory


The Demon Ruins

Description

Past Quelaag's domain we find the Demon Ruins that lead down towards Lost Izalith. The player must first fight Ceaseless Discharge, a large mass of magma protecting his sister's grave but also keeping the lava level too high to reach the lower ruins. Once defeated, the lava lowers to reveal many Taurus and Capra demons to fight in the ancient ruins. After getting through to the large door, the player may take the Daughter of Chaos shortcut to Lost Izalith by donating 30 humanity to the chaos covenant. Otherwise, the player must take on the Demon Firesage, the first of all demons, before facing the Centipede Demon in the lava lake below. Beating the demon or cutting off its tail will result in the player receiving the orange charred ring which allows traversal to the ancient city of Lost Izalith.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Ceaseless Discharge 20,000 Mandatory
Demon Firesage 20,000 Mandatory*
Centipede Demon 40,000 Mandatory*

Mandatory* = Mandatory unless skipped with Daughter of Chaos shortcut



Lost Izalith

Description

The ancient city of Lost Izalith is a lava lake with large, crumbling buildings surrounded by half-dragon creatures filling the lake. Once past the fiery cesspool, the Chosen Undead fights their way up the palace remparts until they have reached the peak. After sliding down into the arena, the player is faced with the Bed of Chaos, mother of all demons. The player must avoid the branch-like arms and holes opening up in the floor as they take out both arms before leaping into the heart of the beast. Once inside, the player slays the Chaos Bug controlling the Bed of Chaos and puts it to rest for good.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
The Bed of Chaos 60,000 Mandatory


The Catacombs

Description

The Catacombs are filled with resurrecting skeletons and necromancers, sure to test any player's durability with their fast and high-damaging attacks. Once the player makes their way into the bottom of the catacombs by using the bridge mechanisms inside, they will face off against the abomination Pinwheel who is a necromancer that was merged together with his wife and child. After defeating Pinwheel, the player will receive the Rite of Kindling which allows them to kindle bonfires to +4 and give the player 20 estus flasks to heal with. This triumph also unlocks arguably the most difficult area of the game, the Tomb of the Giants.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Pinwheel 15,000 Mandatory


Tomb of the Giants

Description

After leaving Pinwheel's coffin, the player will venture into a pitch black abyss walking across pathways with only a small amount of light around them to simulate touch. The player can use a light source such as the skull lantern or the sunlight maggot to see better, but otherwise are left in absolute darkness. Giant and giant-dog skeletons litter every corner of this abyss, including archers who can easily catch the player off guard and knock them off into the many pits within the tomb. After reaching the bottom, the player will finally find light once again as Ash Lake becomes visible on a cliffside path. Once back inside, the player will come up against Nito, the First of the Dead. Nito is accompanied by ressurecting skeletons that can only be killed with holy weapons. Once defeated, Nito drops his lord soul and finishes the player's quest to obtain all four great souls.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Gravelord Nito 60,000 Mandatory


Kiln of the First Flame

Description

The final area of Dark Souls is the Kiln of the First Flame, a barren and desolate landscape caked in ash and debris. The Kiln is where Gwyn once relinked the first flame long ago, and to get there the player must face off against four of Gwyn's black knights that fought against the Bed of Chaos. After defeating them and making their way to the kiln, the Chosen Undead comes face to face with the Lord of Cinder, Gwyn. He is hollowed and animalistic, attacking the player with furious strikes. It is clear Gwyn is not at his full strength, as the Chosen Undead puts him to rest and completes their journey. Will the Chosen Undead link the first flame once again, or perhaps attempt to end the cycle? That much is for you to decide.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Gwyn, Lord of Cinder 70,000 Mandatory


DLC Locations

Sanctuary Garden

Description

After getting the broken pendant from a magical golem inside of the Duke's Archives, the player must kill the Hydra waiting in Darkroot Basin to save Dusk from another golem within the cave on the left hand side. After Dusk is saved, the player must leave and come back to find Dusk gone and a magical portal in the cave. When approached, a giant hand will pull you inside. This takes you back in time to the past in Lordran, particularly into the Darkroot Garden. The player awakens inside a cave that leads out into the Sanctuary Garden. Once there, the player must fight the Sanctuary Guardian to gain passage to the Oolacile Sanctuary.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Sanctuary Guardian 30,000 Mandatory


Oolacile Sanctuary

Description

At the Oolacile Sanctuary the player will meet Elizabeth, who tells the player that Dusk was kidnapped by Manus, Father of the Abyss. Once the player is done resting up, they travel to the Royal Wood in search of the Abyss.



Royal Wood

Description

The Royal Wood may be one of the most compact areas in Dark Souls, with two of the game's best bosses in this area. In the wood lies many Oolacile forest residents and stone knights that are eerily similar to the ones found in Darkroot Garden. Optionally, the player will fight the ancient Black Dragon Kalameet. To do so, the player must be attacked by Kalameet in the basin and then talk to Gough in Oolacile to get an incredible cinematic. The fight against the Dragon of Calamity will pose the most signficiant challenge so far, and test the patience of players. Otherwise, the player will take on the fabled hero of the DLC, Knight Artorias. Artorias has been corrupted by the abyss, his right arm broken and unable to wield his greatshield. Instead, Artorias uses extreme agility and power by one-handing his greatsword in an epic duel between two great warriors. After the dust settles, the Wolf Knight is slain and the player will gain access to the Oolacile Township.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Black Dragon Kalameet 60,000 Optional
Knight Artorias 50,000 Mandatory


Oolacile Township

Description

The Oolacile Township is a city sinking into the abyss. The residents are corrupted by the abyss as they have bloated heads and attack in a crazed frenzy. There are dark magic users as well, throwing high damage magic attacks towards the player. The player will unlock some elevator shortcuts before making their way towards the abyss, ending in a fight against the unique Chained Prisoner enemy. After defeating it, the Chosen Undead makes their way down an elevator shaft into the Chasm of the Abyss.



Chasm of the Abyss

Description

The Chasm of the Abyss is a dark, dingy cavern filled with traces of dark magic and evil. Humanity sprites populate the otherwise empty caves as the player makes their way closer and closer towards the source of the Abyss. There is a secret portion of the cave in which the player can rescue Sif the Great Grey Wolf, as she is found protected by Artorias' greatshield. When they reach the source, the Chosen Undead is confronted by the one and only Manus, Father of the Abyss. Manus has an incredibly fast moveset for how big he his, with hard hitting combos and ranged attacks. After dodging through his swings and dark magic, the player finally defeats Manus and puts his soul to rest. In turn they rescue Princess Dusk who is unconcious and unable to recall your heroic act. After Dusk is saved, the player heads back to Elizabeth to divulge the details and collect their reward before heading back to current day Lordran.

Bosses

Name Souls Required?
Manus, Father of the Abyss 60,000 Mandatory
